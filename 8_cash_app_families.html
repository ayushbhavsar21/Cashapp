Common questions

  • What is a sponsored account?

    A sponsored account allows anyone age 13-17 to use Cash App with approval from a parent or trusted adult. It's free to sign up.

  • What can I do with a sponsored account?

    You can get access to financial tools like Cash Card and investing, while still having guardrails to keep your sponsor in control.

  • What controls does a sponsor have?

    Sponsors are the owners for the accounts they approve. So they can see balances, monthly statements, and real-time transactions. They can also manage permissions on investing and Cash Card spending.

  • Is my Cash balance FDIC insured?

    Yes, all sponsored and sponsor’s Cash balances are FDIC insured up to $250,000 through our bank partner.**

  • How can I keep my account safe?

    It’s a good idea to familiarize yourself with common scams, report phishing attempts, and only send money to people you trust. Make sure to always double check recipient information before sending a payment.
